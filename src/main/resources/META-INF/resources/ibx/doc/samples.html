Samples

These "samples" were created during the development of ibx. They were written as quick/dirty tests of various concepts, so there was very little attention given to making them look nice, or be written efficiently (much like this 'documentation').

However, that does not mean they have no value for someone interested in learning ibx. Although they don't use a consistent approach in implementation (specifically because they were trying to test many different scenarios) they can be looked at to see the kinds of ways ibx can be used.

You can find them in the 'ibx/doc/samples'. Each one has its own folder.
